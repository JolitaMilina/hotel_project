About us

Our Mission

We are committed to provide a 360 degree integrated hotel management service that creates exceptional value. Our mission is to provide outstanding facilities and services to our guests. We are focused on high quality standards in our rooms and all other divisions. We provide a fair return on investment for the owners of this hotel. The management provides a positive working and framework condition so that every employee can fulfil his or her role as a host on a daily basis. We promote talent and are committed to training and further education.

Our beginnings - who are we?

We are the hotel of your dreams! We have been owned by a Lithuanian-Latvian team since the July of 2022, who all came together to create this masterpiece. We are creative, fast-moving and successful and we always strive for development.
